The Science

Three layers of intelligence

Most skin apps rely on a single AI model that makes its best guess. Glowlytics runs three analysis engines simultaneously, each built on peer-reviewed dermatology research.

Layer 1

Precision Imaging

Clinical-grade color and texture analysis measures what your eyes can't see: redness patterns, pigmentation shifts, and skin texture changes. All in milliseconds.

Layer 2

Computer Vision

Custom-trained neural networks evaluate skin structure, hydration, and elasticity while detecting individual blemishes in real-time.

Layer 3

AI Dermatologist

A fine-tuned GPT-4o model classifies conditions, grades severity, and delivers personalized recommendations grounded in clinical guidelines.

FAQ

Common questions

Can Glowlytics replace my dermatologist?

Glowlytics is designed to complement your dermatologist, not replace them. Think of it as a daily check-in between appointments, helping you track what's changing and giving you better data to share with your doctor.

What happens to my photos?

Your photos are analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded. We never store facial images on our servers. Your privacy is non-negotiable.

How accurate is the analysis?

Glowlytics uses three analysis engines running in parallel, including clinical-grade image processing and a fine-tuned AI model, to deliver scores with confidence indicators. Our methods are grounded in peer-reviewed dermatology research.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new user gets a free 7-day trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

What skin types does it work with?

Glowlytics works across all skin types and tones. Our analysis pipeline includes ITA (Individual Typology Angle) calibration to ensure accurate results regardless of melanin levels.
